Getting Started Guide for BIPQuantum

seamlessly begin your journey toward securing and leveraging your creative assets.

Welcome to BIPQuantum, your trusted platform for managing, protecting, and monetizing your intellectual property (IP) using cutting-edge AI and blockchain technology.

Connect to the Platform

Start by visiting dapp.bipquantum.com and clicking
"Try for Free."

01

Seamless Login Options

  • Use Internet Identity (II) or NFID for
    secure access to the platform.

Creating an Internet Identity (II):

  • 1.Visit identity.ic0.app.
  • 2.Click "Create Internet Identity".
  • 3.Follow the on-screen steps to create a
    passkey and link your device.
  • 4.Save your Internet Identity securely for
    future use.

02

Explore AI-Assisted Features

Choose "AI-Assisted IP Creation" for
step-by-step guidance or dive into
"Manual IP Creation" to list your IP
assets directly

The AI IP Bot is designed to assist you with:

  • 1.Understanding IP laws and licenses.
  • 2.Generating a customized BIP certificate
  • 3.Organizing your intellectual property portfolio
    effectively.

Set Up Your Profile

To create an IP, start by setting up your profile:

03

Enter your First Name, Last Name,
Nickname, Specialty (e.g.,
Programmer, Designer), and
Country.

Click "Add User" to complete your
profile.

04

create Your iP

Once your profile is set, you’re ready to create
and list your intellectual property:

Validate your author information
and submit your IP for
certification.

Fill Out Your IP Details:

  • 1.Title of the IP.
  • 2.IP Type (e.g., Copyright, Trademark, Trade Secret)
  • 3.Licenses (select one or multiple, such as SAAS, Virtual
    Reproduction, Meta Use)
  • 4.Upload IP File (max file size: 1.5MB)
  • 5.Creation Date
  • 6.Royalties (recommended range: 1–12%)
  • 7.Publishing Details (optional: specify date and country)

Generate a BIP certificate

05

After completing your IP details, the system will
generate a BIP certificate that serves as
immutable proof of ownership secured on the
blockchain.

06

List Your IP on the Marketplace

Define the value of your IP in BQC
Tokens or a supported
cryptocurrency like ICP, ckBTC, or
ckUSDC.

Add specific royalties for each sale,
ensuring you earn from every
transaction involving your asset.

Decide whether to list your IP
publicly on the marketplace or keep
it private.
